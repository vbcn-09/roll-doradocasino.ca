What are cookies?

Cookies are small digital files saved on your device to enhance web interaction with roll dorado. They allow the system to recognize returning visitors, remember preferences, and maintain login sessions efficiently. Some cookies are session-based and disappear after closing the browser, while others remain stored for future convenience. These files do not contain personally identifying information but assist in delivering tailored content. On rolldorado, cookies are implemented solely to optimize usability and technical performance.

How and why cookies are used

Rolldorado uses different types of cookies for several key purposes: functionality, analytics, and preference management. Functional cookies ensure the website operates smoothly, including account access and transaction processes. Analytical cookies collect anonymized data to improve navigation flow and detect potential usability issues. Preference cookies remember user choices like language or theme, making future visits more consistent. Each cookie type used by roll dorado supports an enhanced, privacy-conscious experience without compromising user control.

Roll dorado prioritizes transparency, giving users the freedom to choose how much data is stored during their visits.
